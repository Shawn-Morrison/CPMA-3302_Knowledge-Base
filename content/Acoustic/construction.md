# Acoustic Guitar Construction

Guitar construction refers to how the [[materials]] come together, and the instrument is designed, assembled, and finished. Some may be constructed differently depending on the [[style]] of music the guitar is used for.

## Main Parts of a Guitar

A standard guitar is built from several core components:

- Headstock
- Neck
- Fretboard
- Body
- Bridge

## Specifics for Acoustic Guitars:

- Hollow body design (some may have a "cutaway")
- Sound hole for resonance
- Bracing system inside the top

## Assembly Process

#### Step-by-step Overview

1. Wood selection and cutting
2. Body shaping and routing
3. Neck carving and fret installation
4. Finishing and painting

> Guitar construction is a blend of craftsmanship and precision engineering, where small adjustments in build can dramatically change playability and tone. Construction can play a role in the [[maintenance]] of a guitar as well.
